Ergasia Platform

Digital submission & management of citizen requests

The Ergasia web application provides a modern digital platform for citizens to submit official requests to public services, upload supporting documents, and monitor the progress of their applications in real time.

Through a secure authentication system, users can manage their requests, schedule appointments, and receive updates, while public employees can efficiently review, process, and complete submitted applications.

Ergasia aims to simplify administrative procedures, improve transparency, and reduce processing time by offering a centralized and user-friendly online service.
